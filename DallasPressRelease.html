This exclusive only once a year event is coming back to the Texas Convention Center April 17 & 18, 2021

The Great American Tiny House Show is a national event tour in 7 cities from coast to coast. On display will be 20 Tiny Homes and structures from builders represented from 3 regional states. (See Sample Photos of Tiny Houses Attached) The current crisis for effective affordable housing and the growing homeless population makes this a timely, exciting and relevant showcase. Additional exhibits will include building equipment, tools, supplies, real estate brokers, accessories, travel destinations and recreational products shown to a projected attendance of over 15,000 local and regional consumers.

THE TEXAS GREAT AMERICAN TINY HOUSESHOW HAS THE DISCTINTION OF BEING THE LARGEST GATHERING OF PROFESSIONALLY BUILT TINY HOUSES IN THE UNITED STATES. This weekend show will have seminars, workshops and over 50 related building and household product companies on display.
